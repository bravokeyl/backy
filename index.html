The advantages when you rent a portable sink

There are many different situations and reasons why anyone, or any business, agency or organization, should consider a portable sink rental. At Rent-A-Portable Sink this is our specialization, and we offer an outstanding selection of options in everything from a full sink for food preparation to a hand washing station. One of the most commonly heard comments with our portable sink rental services is the versatility that this offers over buying or trying to add a hand washing sink using other options. Our prices are very reasonable, and you can select the sink rental of your choice and we will deliver it to your location.

Uses and Ideas

We provide sink rental for a wide variety of different clients and for different needs. A few of the situations where having a sink or a hand washing station is a great idea include:

  • Family gatherings, weddings and special events held outdoors or in facilities without sinks
  • Food trucks and catering services
  • Trade shows
  • Festivals and fairs
  • Schools, daycares and babysitting services
  • Health and medical facilities
  • Demonstrations and booths in malls or other locations

We often get calls for different models of hand washing stations for a variety of these types of events, and we carry a range of different models and sizes to suit any requirements.

Hot and Cold Water

At Rent-A-Portable Sink we offer models that feature self-contained hot and cold water features, and also have a waste tank in the system. This means you don't need any drainage hoses and the area is kept completely contained, safe, and neat even with continual use.
